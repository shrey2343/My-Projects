About the Place

Mahi Homestay is nestled in the Ujjain surrounded by Shipra River. The city is famous for its devotion towards god where many temples are situated. The most famous and religious place The Mahakal Temple is situated in the heart of the city. Designed to blend traditional hospitality with modern comfort, our homestay offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle of busy life. Enjoy breathtaking views, home-cooked meals, and personalized service all under one roof.
